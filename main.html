Paris


Paris, the capital of France, is known for its rich culture, stunning architecture, and romantic ambiance. It's a city that beautifully blends history and modernity, making it one of the most visited destinations in the world.

Top 5 Attractions

  1. Eiffel Tower – The iconic iron tower offering breathtaking views of Paris.
  2. Louvre Museum – Home to the famous Mona Lisa and countless masterpieces.
  3. Notre-Dame Cathedral – A stunning example of Gothic architecture.
  4. Seine River Cruise – Enjoy romantic views of Paris from the water.
  5. Montmartre – A historic area filled with artists and charming cafes.

Must-Try Foods

  • Croissant – A buttery, flaky French pastry.
  • Macarons – Colorful, sweet treats with a crisp shell and soft filling.
  • Escargot – A traditional dish of garlic butter snails.
  • Ratatouille – A hearty vegetable stew full of Provençal flavors.
  • Crêpes – Thin pancakes filled with sweet or savory ingredients.
